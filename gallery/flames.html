This is my gallery containing all the fractal flames I made, fractal flames in a short summary are basic pattern continuously irritated on each other, this gives it a very interesting look when contrasted to the classic fractals such as the Mandelbrots and etc. if you want to make some yourself check out Chaotica, and, Apophysis (if you want to easily get started Chaotica is easer to learn/use but Apophysis is completely free (Choatica limits your max resolution you can render) and Apophysis is need to make more complex fractals, preferably you make the fractals in Apophysis and render in Chaotica, also if you want to run Apophysis and your not on windows you can use WINE). Hope you like my fractal Flames!.
